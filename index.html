About the Workshop

Exploring influential texts, text types, and genres through corpus linguistics

The Delphi Workshop 2025 brings together leading scholars to explore the intersection of corpora and diachrony. This invitation-only event focuses on how digital corpus analysis and computational tools enable researchers to examine text types, genre effects, and the influence of different texts in a more systematic manner.

The workshop provides a unique opportunity to share perspectives on both traditional linguistic and philological approaches, as well as emerging digital methodologies that shed light on language evolution across different historical periods.

Workshop Themes

Influential Texts

Examining the impact of key texts on language development and change

Text Types & Genres

Understanding how different text types and genres shape linguistic patterns

Corpus Methods

Digital corpus analysis and computational tools for diachronic research

Diachronic Change

Patterns of linguistic evolution across historical periods
